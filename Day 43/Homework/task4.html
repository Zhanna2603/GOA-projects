Interesting Facts About Animals

Chapter №1

1. To make a kilogram of honey, a bee must fly around 2 million flowers.

2. Lizards have orange glasses on their eyes because their retinas contain a lot of orange-colored fat droplets. It turns out that these animals have light filters. So lizards see the world differently than we do. And not only lizards. Many birds see what we see in red as green.

3. When Europeans first saw the giraffe, they called it a "camelopard", thinking it was a hybrid of a camel and a leopard.
